Frequently Asked Questions

How many projects have you successfully completed?
I have successfully architected and launched over 25+ projects ranging from e-commerce platforms and SaaS applications to complex ERP systems, mobile apps, and enterprise solutions. My portfolio spans various industries including fintech, healthcare, education, logistics, and e-commerce.
What is your typical project timeline?
Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. Typically: Simple websites take 2-3 weeks, full-stack web applications take 4-8 weeks, mobile apps take 6-12 weeks, and enterprise solutions with custom requirements take 3-6 months. I provide detailed timelines after analyzing your specific requirements.
Do you offer maintenance and support after launch?
Absolutely! I provide comprehensive support packages including bug fixes, security updates, feature enhancements, and performance optimization. I offer monthly, quarterly, and annual support plans tailored to your needs, with 24/7 emergency response available for critical issues.
What is your development process?
I follow Agile methodology with regular sprints, transparent communication, and iterative development. Process includes: Requirements gathering & analysis, Wireframing & prototyping, Development with code reviews, Testing & QA, Deployment, and ongoing support. You'll have weekly updates and can request changes throughout development.
Can you work on existing projects or legacy code?
Yes, I specialize in taking over existing projects, refactoring legacy code, and modernizing outdated systems. Whether you need to add features, fix bugs, or completely rebuild your tech stack, I can handle it. I'll provide a detailed code audit and migration plan before starting.
How do you ensure code quality and security?
I follow industry best practices: clean code principles, automated testing (unit & integration tests), code reviews, security audits, OWASP compliance, encrypted data handling, regular backups, and CI/CD pipelines. All projects use version control, monitoring, and logging for production readiness.
What are your rates and payment terms?
I offer flexible pricing models: hourly rates ($50-150/hour), fixed project pricing, and retainer agreements. I require a 50% deposit to start, with the remainder due on completion. For long-term partnerships, I offer monthly retainer packages with priority support. Payment methods: bank transfer, PayPal, or crypto.
